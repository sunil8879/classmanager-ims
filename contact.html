Official Support Policy

To maintain our commitment to providing high-performance, affordable software, all technical support and issue resolutions are handled strictly through official ONLINE channels only.

Reasons for this policy:

  • Technical Efficiency: Remote screen-sharing allows our developers to diagnose and resolve issues in real-time, eliminating the delays of physical travel.
  • Systematic Logging: Digital communication ensures a permanent "paper trail" of every request, guaranteeing that no issue is overlooked.
  • Data Privacy: Troubleshooting in a controlled cloud environment is significantly more secure for your institute's sensitive data than on-site visits.
  • Resource Optimization: By operating as a remote-first SaaS company, we reduce overhead costs, allowing us to invest more in feature updates for your institute.

⚠️ NOTICE: No physical visits or walk-ins are entertained at our development center.
