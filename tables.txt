		CLASS MANAGER

In this system, institute_id is the "Global Foreign Key" that appears in every table to keep the 500 institutes or more, separated.

1. The Multi-Tenant Global Links

These links ensure that no institute can see another's data.

Table Name		Linked To		Foreign Key (Link)
All 14 Data Tables		profiles		institute_id

2. Student-Centric Links

These links connect student records to the master student profile.

Table Name		Linked To		Foreign Key (Link)
student_details		students		student_id (UUID)
attendance		students		roll_number
payment_history		students		student_id and roll_number
performance_tracking	students		roll_number
exam			students		roll_number
certificates		students		student_id
student_allocations		students		roll_number

3. Teacher & Operation Links

These links connect staff and class schedules.

Table Name		Linked To		Foreign Key (Link)
teachers_attendance	teacher_details	teacher_id
batches			teacher_details	teacher_id
student_allocations		teacher_details	teacher_id
students			batches		batch_time (Logical link)

4. Management & Administrative Tables

These tables are primarily linked to the Institute itself rather than individual students/teachers.

Table Name		Linked To		Foreign Key (Link)
expenses			profiles		institute_id
leads			profiles		institute_id
institute_settings		profiles		institute_id
performance_tracking	teacher_details	teacher_name (Informational link)

Summary of Keys:

institute_id: The "Master Key" for the entire software.

roll_number: The "Student Key" used for all reports (Attendance, Exam, Performance).

teacher_id: The "Staff Key" used for tracking teacher presence and allocations.

student_id: The "Database Internal Key" (UUID) used for core data integrity (e.g., linking basic profile to extended details).

fee management and students table are not linked
