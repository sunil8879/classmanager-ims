ABOUT CLASS MANAGER

The Digital Revolution for Indian Institutes

Our Journey & Vision

Class Manager was born out of a simple observation: coaching institutes in India are the backbone of our education system, yet most are still trapped in the "Paper Age." Founded in 2026, our mission was to create an Institute Management System (IMS) that is as powerful as an enterprise ERP but as simple as a WhatsApp chat. We realized that tutors were spending more time on attendance and fee chasing than on actual teaching. Class Manager changes that narrative by providing a seamless, cloud-based environment where every record is just a click away.

The Problem We Solve

Most small to medium-sized institutes struggle with fragmented data. Fees are recorded in one notebook, attendance in another, and student performance is rarely tracked scientifically. This leads to massive revenue leakage and decreased trust from parents. Class Manager centralizes these operations. By providing a single source of truth, we empower institute owners to identify pending payments instantly and monitor student growth over months and years, ensuring no child or rupee is left behind.

Our Core Values

We believe in Accessibility, Integrity, and Innovation. Accessibility means our PWA works on a ₹5,000 smartphone just as well as a high-end laptop. Integrity means your data is secure and never shared. Innovation means we are constantly integrating AI and ML to predict fee defaults and student performance drops before they happen. We are built in India, specifically for the unique needs of the Indian coaching ecosystem.

Today, Class Manager serves hundreds of institutes across the country, from solo home tutors to large-scale test-prep centers. We aren't just a software company; we are a partner in your growth. Every feature we build, from the ID Card Generator to the automated Fee Reminders, is designed with one goal: to give you back your time so you can focus on what you do best—shaping the minds of the future. As we look toward the future, we remain committed to keeping our technology affordable and cutting-edge, ensuring that even the smallest center in the furthest corner of India has access to world-class management tools.
