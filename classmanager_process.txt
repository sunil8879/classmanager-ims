Now. in simple language these are the steps for operating this software.

1. The owner/admin opens the account by signing up in TAB 2. he gets the institute id in this format (CM-1234)
2. The owner/admin then logs in with his credentials. for the owner, all the 26 tabs are visible to him
3. Suppose, there is a teacher who now wants to join the institute. so, the owner/admin gives the institute id to the teacher and asks him to signup. the teacher signs up and gives the details to the owner/admin
4. The owner/admin then generates the teacher id in the teacher portal in TAB 11 and passes on the teacher id to the teacher
the teacher now can use the teacher id in teacher portal in TAB 11. The Teacher id is in this format : TCH-1234
5. The owner/admin now has to create the Batch in batch management in TAB 5
6. Suppose the parents walk in for enquiry, so for leads management, TAB 9 is to be filled up
7. Now, there is a student who wants to join the institute. the owner/admin again gives him the institute id and asks him to sign up in TAB 2.
8. The student passes on the details to the owner/admin. the owner/admin then generates the roll no of the student in the student management section in TAB 3
The student can now use the roll no in Student portal TAB 12. Roll number is in this format : IMS-2026-1234
9. For inserting scholarship details, TAB 23 is to be filled up
10. The owner/admin now links the teacher and the student in student allocation in TAB 18
11. The owner/admin now has to update the BATCH MASTER in TAB 25
12. The owner/admin now has to update the Fee management section in TAB 4
13. The owner/admin can update the attendance of students in TAB 6 and for teachers attendance in TAB 17
14. For fee reminders, owner/admin can visit TAB 8
15. For messages and notices, the owner/admin can visit TABS 19  & TAB 20
16. For expense tracking, the owner/admin can visit TAB 10.
17. For ID card and certificate generation, the owner/admin can visit TBA 13 & TAB 14
18 For performance tracking of the students, TAB 15 is to be visited
19. For inserting exam details of the students, TAB 16 is to be visited
20. For inserting  alumni details, TAB 21 is to be filled up
21. For inserting Inventory details, TAB 22 is to be filled up
22. For generating PTM schedule, TAB 24 is to be filled up
23. For teacher reviews, TAB 26 is to be filled up
24. For various reports, TAB 7 is to be filled up
25. For leave schedule of teachers, TAB 27 is to be visited
26. For the logged in dashboard or visitor dashboard, u can view TAB 1
