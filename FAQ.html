FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Class Manager IMS.

Is Class Manager a mobile app or a website?
Class Manager is a Progressive Web App (PWA). This means it works like a website but can be installed on your phone or desktop like a regular app without using the Play Store. It is lightweight and fast.
How does the automated Fee Reminder work?
Our system tracks the "Pending Amount" in the Fee Management section. You can simply go to the Fee Reminder System, and with one click, it opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message for the specific student.
Can I generate ID Cards for all students at once?
Yes! By entering the student's mobile number and uploading a photo, the ID Card Generator creates a professional vertical ID card instantly which you can print or save as a PDF.
Is my institute data secure?
Absolutely. We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) with enterprise-grade encryption. Each institute's data is isolated, ensuring that your financial and student records are private and visible only to you.
What is the 'Teacher Portal' for?
The Teacher Portal allows you to manage your staff records, including their qualifications, subjects taught, and salary details. It helps you keep track of which teacher is assigned to which batch.
Can students see their own marks and attendance?
Yes. Students can use the Student Portal by entering their unique Roll Number. They can view their attendance percentage, fee balance, and performance reports for the month.
