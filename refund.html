Refund & Cancellation Policy

The Standard

At Class Manager, we operate a transparent "Try-Before-You-Buy" model. We provide comprehensive live demos to ensure you are fully satisfied with the software's capabilities. Because we provide a digital service that is provisioned immediately upon payment, we maintain a strict refund policy to cover the immediate cloud infrastructure and setup costs incurred by our team.

Eligibility

Refunds are only considered in cases of persistent technical failure where the software fails to perform its advertised core functions and our technical team is unable to resolve the issue remotely within 10 business days. Refund requests based on "change of mind," "unused periods," or "feature preference" are not eligible once the institute's workspace has been activated.

Annual SaaS Policy

Class Manager is billed on an annual basis to ensure uninterrupted managed hosting and support. While you may choose to stop using the service at any time, we do not provide pro-rated refunds for the remaining months of a billing cycle. Upon non-renewal at the end of your term, your account will be suspended, and data will be preserved for 30 days before permanent deletion.

The Process

To report a technical grievance for refund consideration, please email sv@nexusai-group.in with your Transaction ID and a detailed description of the technical blocker. Approved refunds are credited back to the original source within 10-14 working days. Please note that setup fees and administrative charges are non-refundable.

Questions? Contact: sv@nexusai-group.in
